# Dacosta Technologies — brand system

## Positioning

Dacosta Technologies is presented as an engineering company focused on software, infrastructure, automation, architecture, and digital products built with precision.

The brand should feel like a technical signature: quiet, structured, premium, industrial, and reliable.

## Visual direction

- Primary base: black, graphite, off-white.
- Accent range: controlled lime, green, teal, and cyan.
- Avoid: generic neon tech, excessive badges, noisy animations, overdecorated gradients.
- Prefer: geometric composition, structural lines, restrained motion, strong contrast, clear typography.

## Voice

- Precise, direct, technical.
- Company-level, not personal-brand-first.
- Professional without sounding inflated.
- Minimal but not empty.
